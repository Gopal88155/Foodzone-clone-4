Free personal accounts for sharing food diaries with your health professional

Easily share food diaries with other people, saving time avoiding printing or manually reentering food diaries. Shared food diaries easily allow people to collaborate on one diary without having to upload or export anything.

Easily share in depth reports as PDF.

Several in depth reports can be made and exported to PDF or excel. The nutrient report shows the individuals nutrient intake over a selected date range for each of the NRV areas including Estimated Average Requirements (EAR), Recommended Dietary Intake (RDI), Average Intake (AI), Suggested Daily Targets (SDT), Upper Limits (UL), Macronutrients as a percentage of energy intake and Fatty acids as a percentage of fat intake.
